The Tragedy of the Poacher

Cause and Effect Model

This model demonstrates the cause and effect system that is created by zoos, a topic that falls in the category of animal abuse. It is not the main topic that we decided to create our video on, but it certainly pertains to the message of helping animals.

Video

Our project will create the feeling that poaching is wrong because of how innocent and humane the animals look. To create this feeling, we gave them googly eyes, a feature that usually only people have. Our story of a boss of a poaching business realizing how bad it is to poach, and getting a different job. The video will show the audience how morally wrong poaching is, and that there needs to be action taken to stop it.
